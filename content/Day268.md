Starting next month, we'll be upgrading the arrival process at our airport.
To speed up identity verification and improve security, we'll be installing facial-recognition gates at all international arrival checkpoints.
Passengers will simply look into the camera instead of presenting their passports.
This new system also has the added benefit of automatically keeping a record of everyone who enters the country through our terminal.
Because of this change, all airport staff will also need to register their facial data in advance.
Please see Oliver Bennett this week so he can take your photo and complete your registration.
