Thanks for listening to Radio 101.
Riverside's local news station.
Tonight's featured story is the town's plan to renovate the community swimming pool on Cedar Lane.
The facility has been in operation for over 35 years and the equipment is now outdated.
The pool will be converted into a modern aquatic recreation center with spa facilities and wellness areas.
Several designs have been proposed and digital images are available on the town's Web site.
Residents are encouraged to view the designs and vote on their favorite.
Just keep in mind that construction will begin in two months.
